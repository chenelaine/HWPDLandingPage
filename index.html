About the Author

Elaine is a seasoned startup executive with a unique background spanning product development and product management. She has served at the VP level in engineering and product management at several startups, including Rethink Robotics, Zeo, Zeemote and SensAble Technologies. She is currently the founder of ConceptSpring, where she provides strategic product development consulting services to executives and leaders of product teams. She is also a Senior Lecturer at the MIT Sloan School of Management, where she teaches entrepreneurship, primary market research, and leadership development in startup settings. Elaine holds a BS and an MS in mechanical engineering from MIT.
